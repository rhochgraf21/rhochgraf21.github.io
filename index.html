Public Speaking

As a Karate Instructor, I have learned the important skill of public speaking. I have learned "read" audiences, articulate my thoughts in a clear manner, and research and organize ideas.

Programming

I began learning programming in 2013 using the MIT app inventor 2, and I created games, such as Wheel of Fortune and developed a program to read stock data to low vision users. From there, I learned Javascript, Python, HTML/CSS, and Git through Codecademy. In summer of 2016, I set up a website hosted on a raspberry pi to collect and read stock data to low vision users, taught myself PHP, and coded the webpage using the Yahoo Finance API. When the API was shut down, I went and reconstructed for Alpha Vantage API, IEX API, and then moved the system to Google's Dialogflow to integrate with the google assistant. Development continues to this day.

Finance

I have interests in economics as well as stock trading. Economics is a personal hobby, and I enjoy reading books such as The General Theory (Keynes), The Wealth of Nations (Adam Smith), Red Ink (Wessel), and Freakonomics (Levitt, Dubner). As for trading, I have been developing stock trading metrics and machine learing algorithms to assist with trading in order to inform my own trading portfolio.

My First Album
